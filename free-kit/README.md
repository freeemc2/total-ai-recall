## What's in here

- **`CLAUDE.md`** — the startup file. Your AI reads this every time it boots. Fill in who you are and what you're working on.
- **`memory/MEMORY.md`** — the index. The map of everything your AI knows about you, sorted by how often it's needed.
- **`memory/PERSONA.md`** — how you want to be worked with. The single most useful file. Fill it in honestly.
- **`memory/journal.md`** — the shared notepad for running more than one AI session at once. Optional. Ignore it until you need it.
- **`memory/templates/`** — fill-in-the-blank starters for the four kinds of memory: about-you, feedback (corrections), project, reference.

## The 5-minute start

1. Copy `CLAUDE.md` and the `memory/` folder into the root of your project.
2. Open `CLAUDE.md`, fill in the blanks about you and what you're building.
3. Open `memory/PERSONA.md`, tell it how you want to be worked with.
4. Start your AI. It reads everything on startup and boots up knowing you.
5. From now on: when something matters, save it.
